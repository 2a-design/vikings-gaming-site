//community

join the 643 members on our discord!

The Vikings Gaming Discord server is our community's home. It's the main place for community interaction, and for any and all updates or announcements from us.

Here's some of what our Discord server has to offer

  • active gaming community

    Members of our community are always active in the chat and happy to join you for a game or two!

  • announcements + updates

    Our Discord server is the best place to stay up to date on what's going on with Vikings Gaming and our events!

  • weekly online game nights

    We host online game nights every Saturday. Whether it's Lethal Company, Among Us, Roblox, TFT, or anything else, you'll be sure to have fun!

  • minecraft smp server

    We have a survival multiplayer Minecraft server open to all club members! Just join the server and verify to get started!

  • 30+ specific game channels

    Looking to find friends for a specific game? We have active channels for over 30 different games!

  • community marketplace

    Looking to buy or sell some gaming hardware or accessories you don't need anymore? We've got an active secondhand marketplace channel!

OUR TEAMS

We operate collegiate esports teams that compete with schools across the country in various competitive games. These are the games we currently field teams for:

  • League of Legends

  • Valorant

  • Rocket League

Born from the post-covid ashes of the PSU PC Gaming Club, Vikings Gaming seeks to unite both PSU students and the greater community through a shared love of gaming, competition, and community.
